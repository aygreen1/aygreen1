About Me

 

  • I was born in Tokyo, Japan, and moved to the United States as a child. I am an American Citizen and went to grade school in Des Moines, WA,

    and Dallas, TX. After high school, I moved back to Washington state from Texas. I started pursuing a job as a CNC machinist for a company named Exact Aerospace, where

    I found an interest in programming and machinery. After three years, I joined the Army for six years and worked splitting the time between special forces and an airborne

    infantry unit. After my military contract ended, I enrolled in Wake Tech community college and bought a house in Clayton, NC.
